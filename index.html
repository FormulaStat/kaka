About GlobalstoxFX

Founded in 2011, GlobalstoxFX is a licensed and regulated platform dedicated to secure, transparent, and accessible crypto investment. We combine advanced blockchain technology with strict AML and KYC compliance to protect investors at every step — from two-factor authentication and cold wallet storage to audits and insurance-backed safeguards.

Our solutions serve all levels, from Starter Mining Plans to Pro Packages and institutional strategies. With real-time reporting, transparent metrics, and global support, we don’t just provide services — we empower investors to grow confidently in the digital economy.

  • Security – Military-grade systems, cold wallets, and insured assets.
  • Transparency – Regular audits, regulatory compliance, and real-time data.
  • Global Reach – Serving clients in multiple countries with multilingual support.
  • Innovation – Harnessing cutting-edge blockchain and mining technologies.
